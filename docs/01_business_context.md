# 01_business_context.md

## Problem

A bank wants to reduce customer churn.

Goal:

* Predict which customers are likely to churn
* Identify key drivers of churn
* Support retention strategies

---

## Business Questions

* What is the overall churn rate?
* Which customer segments churn the most?
* Which behaviors indicate churn risk?
* Can we identify high-risk customers early?

---

## Output Expectations

* Actionable insights (not just statistics)
* Clear segments with higher churn
* Interpretable model outputs
* Business-friendly reporting

---

## Important Notes

* Correlation is not causation
* Avoid overclaiming insights
* Focus on explainability
