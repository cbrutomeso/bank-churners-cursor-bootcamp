Read `README.md` and `docs/03_success_criteria.md`.

Assume the outputs from the earlier project stages already exist.
If critical upstream artifacts are missing, identify the gap before drafting the final report.

Then review the available project outputs, including:
- EDA notebook and figures
- modeling outputs and metrics
- Streamlit app functionality
- BI tables, figures, and executive summary

Create a final report in `reports/final_report.md`.

Requirements:
1. Reuse existing evidence, tables, and figures when relevant instead of recreating everything
2. Include: executive summary, business problem, EDA insights, modeling approach, model performance, churn drivers, high-risk customer insights, limitations, recommendations, and next steps
3. Use business-friendly language and avoid unnecessary technical detail
4. Support key statements with evidence from the project outputs
5. Avoid overclaiming causality

Keep the report concise, clear, and useful for decision-making.
